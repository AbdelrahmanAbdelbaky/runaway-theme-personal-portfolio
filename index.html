ProjectsMy Projects

Step into my world of creation and innovation as you explore the projects section of my portfolio. Each project is a testament to my boundless curiosity and my desire to make a positive impact.

Orbital Dynamics

This project involves creating a simulation of a Binary Star system using the programming language Python and the physics engine Vpython. The simulation will likely include graphical representations of the two stars and their orbits, as well as the ability to manipulate and observe the system under different conditions and parameters. The goal of the project is likely to gain a better understanding of the dynamics and behavior of Binary Star systems, and potentially make predictions about real-world binary star systems.

AquaClimate

The effects of climate change is substantial and wreaking destruction in all facets. This project was developed to use the technology of IoT in mitigating those effects. The project aims to cope with this problem in Egypt by monitoring dynamics of climate change in the Mediterranean Sea with a system of sensors to gather data of air humidity, temperature, and particular matter. The collected data is implemented methods of interpretation, including statistical and predictive analysis using linear regression.

Solar Explorer

This simulation includes graphical representations of the sun, planets, and other celestial bodies, as well as their orbits and movements. The user will be able to manipulate and observe the solar system under different conditions and parameters, such as changing the speed of time, zooming in and out, and viewing the system from different perspectives. The goal of the project is to gain a better understanding of the dynamics and behavior of the solar system, and to explore the relationships between the different celestial bodies.

Anti-Irritant Hygiene

This project was developed with the aim of fine-tuning the process of manufacturing hand sanitizers, minimizing the number of aberrations and disturbances in the products. This is achieved by use of an automated feedback control system, which includes a load cell to measure the mass of the mixture and determine the amount of alcohol needed, as well as a pump to add it. Additionally, the project is attached to a dynamic solar energy system to provide the system with the required power.

The Frost generator

The overarching aim of the project was focused on the problem of Clean Water Resources. The project is an atmospheric water generator that condenses atmospheric water vapor and then convert it to a suitable irrigation water. The idea of the project is to use the cool refrigeration coils to pull humidity out of the air and condense it into droplets of water. The success of the project in producing water that is safe and sustainable gives it a strong alignment with Egypt's 2030 vision.

Limbs savior

Imagine a world where heavy industry is safe and secure for workers, where the threat of injury or death is greatly diminished. This is the vision behind this project, devised to prioritize the safety of workers. The concept at its core is a pair of gloves, designed to be worn by the operator. These gloves are equipped with wireless technology that connects them to the machinery and establishes a virtual boundary of safety. Once the gloves breach this boundary, the machine is automatically disabled.

Micromouse Robot

In a try to keep up with the most recent technological advancements of this decade and the possible future development trajectory of AI and robots, this micro robot was developed. This micro robot was created using mathematics and programming knowledge with the goal of running through various sorts of mazes and solving them as rapidly as feasible.

EcoRide

Are you ready to join the fight against pollution? This project aims to tackle pollution by focusing on the increase of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere. The proposed solution involves a chemical reaction in car exhaust pipes to collect and convert harmful gases from the atmosphere and exhausts into pure carbon dioxide. This carbon dioxide is then converted into hydrogen gas through an electrochemical reaction, which produces electricity to power the car's motor.
